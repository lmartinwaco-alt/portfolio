Collection of Planning Documents

These documents detail the analysis, design, and evaluation steps I take to ensure instructional projects are learner-centered and effective. Click on any document title below to view the artifact.

Documents for Review:

  • Training Needs Analysis (TNA) : Identifies needs of learners.
  • Usability Testing : Plans to test the first iteration of the learning unit.
  • Learner Surveys & Interviews : Instruments used along with TNAs to design learning.
  • Empathy Maps & Personas : Detailed profiles of target learners to inform design decisions.
  • Storyboards : Visual blueprint for content and interactions
